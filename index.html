Sometimes you just want to take a break without texting ten different people. Send a ping to see which friends are around right now. You only share your location when you explicitly want to.

ENG: SHA-256 Client Hashing

Choose Your Real Circle

We don't assume everyone in your phonebook is a friend. When you join, you explicitly select who you actually want to hang out with. They won't know you selected them unless they select you back.

ENG: Ephemeral Redis Engine

Zero Persistent Tracking

We don't keep a permanent map of where you've been. When you turn on discovery mode to find friends, your ping lasts for exactly 15 minutes and then vanishes from the system entirely.

ENG: BSSID Intersection Mapping

Proof You Are Actually There

No fake locations or remote stalking. The app uses localized network signals to mathematically verify that people are actually in the exact same physical area before they can match with you.

ENG: Trust Bridge State Machine

The "Friend of a Friend" Safety Net

When you expand your search outside your immediate circle, you stay protected. If a friend-of-a-friend is nearby, you only see a gentle notification saying "A friend of [Sarah] is nearby." Your identity is only revealed if you both hit accept.
